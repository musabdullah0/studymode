About Study Mode

Study Mode is a app that helps you find where your friends are studying, so you can spend less time goofing off and more time working.

This is a progressive web app which means that if you are on a mobile device, you can press 'Add to Home Screen' and be able to access Study Mode as if it were a normal mobile app you downloaded on the App Store. It can even work (with limited capabilites of course) offline!
